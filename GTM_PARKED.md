# GTM Parked

**Status:** All items below are paused until VIBE North Star v7.1 ships.
**Last updated:** 2026-04-15
**Owned by:** Bill Lupo

This document tracks every go-to-market activity that is currently paused.
Each item has a restart condition. When v7.1 ships (target May 9), this
document gets reviewed and items move back to active GTM planning.

The principle: VIBE does not market a product that has not yet demonstrated
its core promise. The core promise is the autonomous loop. Until that loop
runs in production for real customers, marketing it is premature and
counter-productive — it sets expectations that the product cannot yet meet
and creates support load that distracts from finishing the build.

## 4. Risk acknowledgment

Parking GTM has costs:

- **Pipeline cools.** Existing prospects may go cold over 3-4 weeks of
  silence. Mitigation: light-touch maintenance ("we're heads down on a major
  release, expect updates in 4 weeks").
- **Competitor advantage.** If Lovable, Bolt, v0, or Retool ship something
  comparable in this window, the autonomous OS wedge gets harder to claim.
  Mitigation: ship faster.
- **Cash runway pressure.** Reg-A and investor conversations stall.
  Mitigation: this is acceptable because closing the autonomous loop makes
  the next investor conversation worth 3x the one happening today.

The decision to park GTM is a deliberate trade: short-term pipeline coolness
for long-term product proof. The product proof, once it exists, makes every
subsequent GTM activity dramatically more effective.
